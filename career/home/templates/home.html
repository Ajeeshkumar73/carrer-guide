About

AI-Powered Career Guidance

Our platform helps students choose the right career path with confidence. It analyzes academic performance, personal interests, and skills to recommend suitable fields like engineering, medicine, design, or management.

Students get personalized career suggestions after key milestones such as +2 or undergraduate studies. They can also connect with communities to share resources, learn from experts, and explore new opportunities.

With a built-in AI voice assistant, users enjoy hands-free support that guides them through career options, educational needs, job prospects, and skill development paths.
